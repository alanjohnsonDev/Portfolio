Fit & Frosted

Protein Donuts Landing Page

Fit & Frosted is a Protein Donut small business that was founded in Southern Maryland. One of my gym friends needed a page to push her business to, inlcuding all the flavors, prices, and where and how to order. She gave me her ideas of the page, and I sent her this page and she loved the colors (which matched her logo), the presense of all the flavors and how its simple and easy to read.

The things I learned through this project is how to make a table using HTML and CSS. How to overcome my fear of finishing a project. Also, gravitating towards other color schemes and using gradients for the background colors using CSS.

The challenges I had on this project was trying to find the right color pallete for my color scheme. My client has a colorful logo and I wanted to try and throw in as many colors from the logo as possible. I am a perfectionist, so trying to make this page as simple as possible was hard for me, but I understood what she wanted and she loves the finished product.
